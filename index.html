Am I eligible?

Eligibility for executive MBA

Most exec MBA programs are built for working professionals, not freshers. Here's what to expect across institutions.

  • ✓
    Bachelor's degree from any UGC-recognised university
  • ✓
    Minimum 3 years full-time work experience (5–10 years for IIM/XLRI senior programs)
  • ✓
    Currently employed — most programs require active employment
  • ✓
    No CAT or GMAT required for most executive programmes
  • ✓
    Age 25–55 — designed for mid-to-senior career professionals
  • ✓
    English proficiency — all programs delivered in English

Frequently asked

Common questions about executive MBA

An Executive MBA is designed for working professionals with 3–15 years of experience. You study on weekends and continue your job. A regular MBA is a 2-year full-time program requiring CAT/GMAT, suited for freshers or professionals with 0–3 years of experience looking to change career direction.
No — most exec MBA programs require you to be employed while studying. Classes are on weekends (Saturday and Sunday) with some online sessions on weekday evenings. You keep drawing your salary throughout the entire duration.
IIM executive programmes award an executive certificate or diploma — different from the flagship PGP which requires CAT. For professionals with 8–15 years of experience, the IIM executive credential is widely respected and often more relevant than the PGP, given its applied curriculum and senior peer cohort.
It depends on your experience and goals. For 10+ year professionals at senior levels, IIM Ahmedabad SMP and IIM Calcutta EPBM are most prestigious. For 5–8 year professionals, IIM Kozhikode, XLRI and SPJIMR are excellent options. For broader access with a UGC degree, top private university online MBA programs are strong and affordable.
Online MBA programs: ₹2–5 lakh. IIM executive programmes: ₹5–12 lakh. XLRI: ₹8–10 lakh. ISB PGP: ₹35–40 lakh. ISB CXO: ~₹5.5 lakh. MDI and SPJIMR: ₹7–10 lakh. Most programs offer EMI payment options ranging from ₹10,000 to ₹80,000/month.
Yes. Under UGC-DEB regulations, several universities now offer fully online MBA degrees equivalent to campus programs for employment. IIM programs award executive certificates/diplomas (not degrees), but these carry enormous brand value. For government jobs or immigration, opt for a UGC-DEB approved degree program.
