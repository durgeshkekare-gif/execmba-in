Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

execmba.in ("we", "our", "the site") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information we collect

  • Contact information you submit through our enquiry and counselling forms — name, phone number, email address, work experience and program interest.
  • Usage data — pages visited, time on site, device type, browser, and referral source, collected via Google Analytics 4.
  • Cookies — session cookies for site functionality and analytics cookies for usage tracking. You can opt out via your browser settings.

How we use your information

  • To connect you with a program advisor who can provide personalised guidance on executive MBA programs.
  • To send you relevant program information and updates by email or phone, based on your stated interests.
  • To improve our website content, user experience and program listings.

How we share your information

Your contact details submitted through counselling forms are shared with our program advisory partners who contact you to provide guidance. We do not sell your data to third parties, and we do not share your data with advertisers.

Data retention

We retain your contact information for up to 24 months from your last interaction with us. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at privacy@execmba.in.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may opt out of communications at any time by replying "unsubscribe" to any email or by contacting us directly.

Contact

For privacy-related queries: privacy@execmba.in
