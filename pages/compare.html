Decision guide — who should pick what

Choose IIM Ahmedabad SMP if...

You have 10+ years experience, are already at VP / Director level, want the highest-prestige credential in India, and are targeting CXO-level in 2–3 years.

Choose IIM Kozhikode EMBA if...

You have 5–9 years experience, want an IIM credential without the steep 10-year requirement, and prefer a longer 18-month program with strong campus modules.

Choose XLRI EMBA if...

You are in HR, people management, or general management. XLRI's values-based education and alumni network are unmatched for HR professionals targeting CHRO roles.

Choose ISB CXO if...

You are 10+ years in, want a shorter program, value ISB's global ranking and network, and are comfortable with a mostly online format with selective campus visits.

Choose IIT program if...

You are a tech professional (engineer, IT manager, product manager) targeting tech leadership, product or strategy roles. IIT credentials are unbeatable in the tech sector.

Choose Online MBA if...

You have 3–5 years experience, want a UGC-recognised degree (not just a certificate), need the lowest EMI, or want the flexibility of a fully online format with no campus visits.
