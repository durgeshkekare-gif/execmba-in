The real question: degree vs certificate while working?

Choose a degree (Online MBA / IIM Nagpur Blended MBA) if: you need a formal MBA degree credential for government jobs, immigration (WES), PhD admissions, or roles where "MBA" is a stated qualification requirement.
Choose a certificate (IIM/IIT executive programmes) if: you want faster career impact (6–12 months vs 2 years), you already have a degree and just need the IIM credential for promotion/salary discussion, and your employer doesn't require a formal degree.

IIM Nagpur Blended MBA — the working professional's IIM degree

The IIM Nagpur Blended MBA for Working Professionals is uniquely designed for the "keep working" scenario: weekday evening live sessions (so you work 9–6 and study 7–9 PM), mandatory campus modules at IIM Nagpur (12–15 days total per year — use leaves), full UGC MBA degree on completion. No career gap. CAT or GMAT required for admission.

Online MBA while working — how to manage time

  • Block 2 hours daily: 7–9 PM on weekdays is enough for most online MBA programmes (10 hrs/week)
  • Use commute time: Pre-recorded lectures can be consumed during commute (2–3 hrs recouped)
  • Tell your manager: Most companies support MBA study — some reimburse fees. Being open avoids last-minute assignment panic
  • Assignment batching: Don't spread assignments — complete a week's work in one Sunday sitting (3–4 hours focused is better than 1 hour daily)
  • Choose online exams: All top online MBA programmes have online exams at authorised centres or online-proctored from home
