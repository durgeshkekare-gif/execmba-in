IIM Ahmedabad AGMP vs IIM Kozhikode New Age Management 2026 — Which to Choose?

The two most popular IIM executive programs

IIM Ahmedabad AGMP and IIM Kozhikode New Age Management are the most enquired-about IIM executive programs for senior professionals in 2026. Both are NIRF top-3. Both run 6–9 months. Both include campus immersion. The choice matters.

~₹3.5–4L + GST, 4–6 months, campus at IIMA Ahmedabad. NIRF #1, AACSB, EQUIS, FT ranked. Highest brand-recognition executive credential in India. Best for consulting, large corporate and board-level hiring contexts. Enroll here →

IIM Kozhikode New Age Management

~₹2–3L + GST, ~9 months, campus at IIMK Kochi. NIRF #3, Triple Crown. Digital disruption and new-age business focus. EQUIS + AMBA gives stronger international recognition. ₹1L+ cheaper than AGMP. Enroll here →
