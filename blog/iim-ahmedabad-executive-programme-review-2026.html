IIM Ahmedabad Executive Programme (AGMP) 2026 — Complete Review

What is IIM Ahmedabad's AGMP?

The Accelerated General Management Programme (AGMP) from IIM Ahmedabad is a short-duration executive management programme designed for senior professionals. IIM Ahmedabad (NIRF #1 Management, AACSB accredited) is India's most prestigious management institution — an AGMP credential from IIMA carries significant weight in India's corporate and entrepreneurship ecosystem. The programme is typically 4–6 months with live weekend sessions and a mandatory campus immersion module at IIMA's iconic campus in Ahmedabad.

Fee is approximately ₹3.5–4L + GST. This positions it as an accessible entry point to the IIM Ahmedabad credential for working professionals who cannot commit to the full-time MBA or PGPX programme.

Who should apply?

AGMP is designed for mid-to-senior professionals with 8–15 years of work experience targeting senior management and leadership roles. Typical profiles: Department heads and senior managers at large organisations, entrepreneurs and founders wanting structured general management exposure, and senior professionals in technical or functional roles (finance, engineering, operations) moving into general management responsibilities.

Curriculum

AGMP covers core general management: strategy and competitive analysis, financial management and value creation, marketing strategy and brand management, operations and supply chain, leadership and organisational behaviour, digital strategy and technology management, and economics for managers. The programme uses the IIM Ahmedabad case study pedagogy — the same approach used in the full-time MBA — giving participants exposure to the IIMA teaching method that has produced much of India's corporate leadership.

Campus immersion — what to expect

The IIMA campus immersion is one of the most valued aspects of the AGMP. The Louis Kahn-designed campus in Ahmedabad is internationally recognised. Immersion typically runs for 3–5 days and includes faculty-led sessions, peer interactions, alumni networking events and visits to the IIMA library and case study archives. Alumni consistently cite the campus experience and peer cohort as the highest-value aspects of the programme.

Is AGMP worth the fee?

For professionals who can justify the ₹3.5–4L investment, the IIM Ahmedabad credential has measurable career impact. In India's corporate market, "IIM Ahmedabad" on a LinkedIn profile or resume consistently attracts recruiter attention for senior management roles. The main question is whether your current career trajectory makes general management training relevant — if you are already in a general management role or targeting one, the AGMP ROI is strong. If you are in a highly specialised technical role with no near-term management ambition, a more technical IIT program may deliver better returns.
